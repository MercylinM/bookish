Be a Full Person

The first suggestions in Adichie’s manifesto goes beyond simplistics notions of feminism.
It’s about liberating women from the expectations that motherhood, or anyother predefined role,
should consume thier entire being. It’s a call to action to live a life rich in diverse experiences,
passions and contributions beyond the domestic sphere.This is not just about “having it all,”
but about consciously choosing what”all” means for you and rejecting the societal pressure to
conform to a narrow definition of womanhood. It is about a well rounded life with different
characteristics to be able to give her daughter an idea on what a woman actually can be.

Challenging Traditional Gender Roles: There’s a growing awareness of the harmful effects of rigid
gender roles , particulary on women. This includes the expectation that women should be primarily
caregivers, proritize their families’ needs above their own and suppress their ambitions.
Adichie’s suggestion directly challenges these stereotypes.
Example you could include content drinking common myths about womens roles , such as the idea
that women are naturally more nurturing or that they are less ambitious than men.
