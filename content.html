Written By

Bright Karanja

The Color of Laughter: Aims to capture both the significant role of race in Noah's narrative and the surprising and effective use of humor as a tool for survival and understanding. It suggests a story where serious issues are explored with intelligence and a disarming sense of humor.

The Rebel’s Colorblind Son

Written By

Mercylin Muthoni

The Rebel’s Colorblind Son reflects the core of Trevor Noah’s story, a boy born into a divided world, taught to see beyond its lines by a fiercely independent mother. Patricia's rebellion against the system shaped Trevor’s values, humor, and identity. This title honors their bond and the way he inherited her fighting spirit in a society obsessed with labels.
